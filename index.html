Khushi Jain

Khushi Jain is the founder and creative powerhouse behind OM (ओuthentic मedia). With a Bachelor of Commerce in Marketing, Innovation & Entrepreneurship from the University of Auckland, New Zealand, she brings an international perspective and a deep passion for transforming ideas into impact. Khushi started OM with a simple belief: Creativity is a skill, and it’s how she solves problems. Khushi is also deeply committed to using her talents for a greater purpose. Beyond her commercial work, she regularly collaborates with NGOs, community initiatives, and small grassroots organisations, providing her expertise in marketing, design, and strategy on a pro bono basis. Whether creating content, mentoring teams, or amplifying important causes, Khushi believes that every project is an opportunity to make a meaningful difference.
